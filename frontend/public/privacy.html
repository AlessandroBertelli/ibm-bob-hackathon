Privacy

100% free, non-commercial open-source study project. No monetisation, no advertising. We count anonymised visits and sign-ins for internal statistics — no cookies, no fingerprinting, no sharing with third parties.

Last updated: 7 May 2026.

Specifically

  • We store your email only if you sign in as a host (Supabase Auth uses it to send your magic-link).
  • We store the meals you save against your account so you can re-use them.
  • We auto-delete sessions after 30 days.
  • Account deletion is one click in your profile menu. Removes the auth user immediately; saved recipes survive as anonymous entries so other people's sessions don't break.
